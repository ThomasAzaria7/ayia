Ayia means ‘wild’ or ‘in the bush’ in Ma’di. It is a natural skin brand that uses native and exotic botanical. The skin line is formed to support the current gap of skin care for dark skin woman. The intention for the brand is to create products that are dark skin conscious and environmental friendly. It is an herbal based brand that intends to gather herbs from around the world to create the ultimate skin care. At Ayia we hope to work outside the box and create products that are ancient by ingredients and modern by name for woman who are willing to invest in their skin and health.
